Popular Tunisian Dishes

Couscous

Tunisian couscous is a traditional Berber dish of steamed semolina served with vegetables, meat, or fish.

Brik

A crispy pastry filled with egg, tuna, parsley and capers, typically fried and served as an appetizer.

Lablabi

A hearty chickpea soup flavored with garlic, cumin, and olive oil, often served with bread and poached eggs.

Ojja (Shakshuka)

Spicy tomato and pepper stew with eggs, often prepared with merguez sausage or seafood.
